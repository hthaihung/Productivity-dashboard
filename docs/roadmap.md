\# Roadmap



\## North star



Build a modular dashboard that helps one person think clearly, execute consistently, and stay motivated across study and work.



\## Core pillars



\- Capture: notes, quick ideas, inbox

\- Execution: tasks, projects, deadlines

\- Focus: pomodoro, focus sessions, distraction resistance

\- Reflection: journaling, reviews, analytics

\- Motivation: streaks, rewards, XP, badges

\- Recreation: short, intentional break-time features



\## Release framing



\### V1 – foundation



\- Auth and onboarding

\- Home dashboard

\- Notes

\- Todos

\- Pomodoro

\- Streaks

\- Settings



\### V1.5 – productivity layer



\- Projects

\- Calendar

\- Reminder system

\- Search

\- Tags and organization



\### V2 – intelligence and motivation



\- Weekly reviews

\- Recommendations

\- Smart prioritization

\- Rewards economy

\### V3 – ecosystem



\- Social accountability

\- Shared rooms / study sessions

\- Plugin modules

\- Mini games
